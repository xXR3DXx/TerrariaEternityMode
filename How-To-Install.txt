1) Open TModLoader
2) Go to "Mods"
3) Click "Show Mods Folder"
4) Open the folder called "ModPacks"
5) Drag and Drop "EternityMode.json" into the ModPack folder
6) Open Modpacks in-game (bottom right button in mod folder)
7) Click "Enable only this list"
8) If you don't already have these mods downloaded, there will be a button that will tell you to do so.
9) Join a world and you're good to go!
